I am currently a Professor of CPS security at the Guizhou University and Research Affiliate at the State Key Laboratory of Public Big Data. I graduated with a PhD in control system security from Zhejiang University in June 2020 (Co-supervised by Prof. Jiming Chen and Prof. Peng Cheng).

My research interests include security of cyber-physical system, applied cryptography, mobile computing, and machine learning security.
